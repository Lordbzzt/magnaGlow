Who We Are 

Magna Glow Sdn. Bhd. (MGSB) is a wholly owned Bumiputera company established on 10th February 2000. Over the years, we have built a solid reputation as a trusted Building and Civil Engineering Contractor in Sarawak. With our extensive experience and strong commitment to excellence, we continue to deliver innovative, high-quality, and sustainable construction solutions across both the public and private sectors.

As a UPKJ Class A and CIDB G7 certified contractor with ISO 9001:2008 accreditation, MGSB upholds the highest standards of quality and professionalism in every project we undertake.

Our Commitment

At Magna Glow, we believe in creating value through integrity, innovation, and efficiency.

Our team of qualified engineers, quantity surveyors, and management professionals work together to ensure every project is executed on time, within budget, and beyond expectations. We take pride in our ability to manage diverse projects — from infrastructure development to building works — while maintaining safety and environmental sustainability as top priorities.

Mission

We are committed to delivering reliable construction, renovation, and infrastructure projects through skilled workmanship, strong partnerships, and responsible leadership. Our mission is to create value for clients, empower our team to grow, uphold safety and integrity in every project, and contribute to the progress of Sarawak’s communities.

Vision

To become Sarawak’s most trusted and innovative construction and infrastructure partner, delivering safe, sustainable, and high-quality solutions that improve communities and enable long-term development.

Our Expertise

Magna Glow has successfully completed a wide range of projects, including:

  • Government and municipal buildings
  • Health and educational facilities
  • Roads and infrastructure development
  • Commercial and residential developments
  • Interior and renovation works
