Board of Directors

Murphy Nyegang Anak Nelson Mujah

Managing Director

Bachelor of Civil Engineering, Swinburne University

More Details

Mr. Murphy is an accomplished construction and engineering professional with extensive experience in major infrastructure projects. He began his career as a Senior Tunnel Engineer with MMC Corporation Berhad, contributing to the Klang Valley MRT Project from 2011 to 2017, particularly the tunnelling works between Pudu and Pasar Seni. From 2017 to 2020, he served as Assistant Construction Manager for the MRT SSP Line, where he played a key role in delivering the underground Titiwangsa Station. His responsibilities included project management, subcontractor coordination, and stakeholder engagement, demonstrating strong technical capability and effective leadership.

He holds a Bachelor of Engineering (Civil) with Upper Second Class Honours from Swinburne University of Technology Australia and is a registered member of the Board of Engineers Malaysia. Currently pursuing an MBA at Swinburne Sarawak, Mr. Murphy combines engineering expertise with strategic leadership skills. With proven problem-solving abilities, strong communication, and a commitment to quality, he brings a well-rounded and professional approach to every project he undertakes.
