OUR
STORY

MAGNA GLOW SDN BHD is a wholly owned Bumiputera Company and has established a reputation as a reliable Building and Civil Engineering Contractor in the State of Sarawak. It has the capabilities of providing a full range of diversified construction works by itself or in joint venture with local and international contractors. 

MAGNA GLOW SDN BHD was incorporated on 10th February 2000 with an Authorized Capital of RM 10,000,000.00 and Paid-up Capital of RM 1,000,000.00.

Since its incorporation MAGNA GLOW SDN BHD has successfully undertaken several projects for the government, statutory bodies and private sectors.

The company is currently registered with Pusat Khidmat Kontrator (PKK) Class A, as a Taraf Bumiputera company and Construction Industry Development Board (CIDB) Class G7.

The Company has comprehensive network of supporting team manned by their highly qualified, well trained and experienced technical and management personnel which the company is in the position to ensure effective, efficient and consistently high quality services in order to achieve the kind of return as expected by the Clients.
