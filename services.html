Our 
Services. 

At Magna Glow Sdn. Bhd., we take pride in being a trusted name in the engineering and construction industry. Guided by our core values of integrity, innovation, and excellence, we deliver comprehensive solutions that meet the highest standards of quality and technical precision. Our multi-disciplinary expertise allows us to undertake complex projects across the civil, structural, building, water, and mechanical sectors with proven reliability.

As a CIDB Grade G7 and Class A registered contractor, we are fully equipped to execute projects of any scale and complexity. From initial design and planning to construction and maintenance, our commitment is to provide safe, sustainable, and value-driven engineering outcomes for our clients.

  • Sewerage Systems and Sewer Network Construction
  • General Civil Engineering Works

Road & Earth Works

Magna Glow Sdn Bhd provides dependable road and earth works, including site preparation, earthmoving, drainage, and road construction. With CIDB G7 and UPKJ Class A certifications, we deliver durable and safe infrastructure—even in challenging terrains—ensuring long-lasting access and connectivity for communities.

Scope of Work & Class A Road and Earth Works Head:

  • Earthworks (Levelling, Site Clearing, and Road Preparation)
  • Road Works (Asphaltic and Bituminous Coatings)
  • Construction and Maintenance of Sewerage Networks
  • Footpath and Plankwalk Construction
  • Road Lining, Railing, and Associated Works
  • Construction, Repair, and Maintenance of Road Kerbs and Dividers
  • Connection of Private Pipes to Sewerage Systems
